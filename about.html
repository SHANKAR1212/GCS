An ISO 9001:2008 Certified Company

General Crop Science Pvt Ltd was founded by an experienced industry expert and two promising partners. With two decades of valuable experience, GCS is a significant player in various business areas, including technical services, corporate sales, contract manufacturing, domestic brand sales, international trade, and P2P toll formulation services for multinational companies. We are recognized as top suppliers of Glyphosate and Emamection formulations in both the Indian and global markets.

Supplier of Glyphosate & Glufosinate across the globe

The company has factories in Panipat (Haryana) and Ghiloth (Rajasthan).Products include insecticides, Herbicides, fungicides,biostimulants, plant growth regulators etc.. GCS focuses on coming up with new ideas and solutions. They have their own research team and a dedicated group of agriculture experts to create many new products regularly.
