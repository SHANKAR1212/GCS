OVERVIEW

General Crop Science Pvt. Ltd.

  • Established as an ISO 9001:2015 certified agrochemical company with over two decades of industry experience.
  • Diverse presence in multiple business verticals, encompassing Technical, Intermediate Corporate Sales, Contract Manufacturing, Domestic Brand Sales, International Trade, and P2P Toll Formulation Business for leading MNCs.
  • Renowned for being a top supplier of Glyphosate and Glufosinate formulations in both the Indian and international markets.
  • Operates state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in Panipat (Haryana) and Ghiloth (Rajasthan), with a focus on producing insecticides, herbicides, fungicides, biostimulants, plant growth regulators, and more.
  • Strong commitment to innovation, boasting in-house R & D capabilities and a dedicated team of agriculture specialists for continuous product development.
  • Offers comprehensive registration support, including Data Generation and Dossier Development, with GLP data available.
  • Customized formulation capabilities, such as ZC, Me, ULV, tailored to customer requirements.
  • Extensive global reach, serving as a manufacturer and supplier of Glyphosate to more than 25 countries.
  • Proudly boasts a portfolio of 300+ innovative formulations, emphasizing diversity and innovation.
  • Operates a fully accredited Formulation Plant and Contract Manufacturing Business in Panipat, Haryana, featuring carefully segregated production lines for purity and quality assurance.
  • Employs a team of experienced scientists to develop, test, and optimize new products and processes, with a strong focus on maintaining product quality.
  • Ensures rigorous quality assurance from sourcing raw materials to the final product, including testing at an ISO 17025-accredited laboratory.
  • Technical manufacturing capabilities include a Fully DCS operated Zero Liquid Discharge Plant, a Pilot Plant Facility, In-House Research Centre, Backward Integrations, and proximity to a seaport, facilitating efficient export operations.

This overview reflects our strong commitment to excellence in our domestic operations, with a focus on quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
