OVERVIEW

General Crop Science Pvt. Ltd. is a prominent agrochemical company that has earned recognition as a Government-recognized Export House. With over two decades of experience, GCS stands as an industry leader with a strong presence across various business verticals, including Technical, Intermediate Corporate Sales, Contract Manufacturing, Domestic Brand Sales, International Trade, and P2P Toll Formulation Business for multinational corporations.

At the heart of GCS is a commitment to research and development, bolstered by a dedicated team of highly qualified scientists, agriculture specialists, process engineers, chemists, and quality control and quality assurance professionals. This robust R&D capability empowers us to develop and commercialize new products tailored to the specific requirements of our customers.

GCS boasts a state-of-the-art GLP CRO facility in Ghiloth (Rajasthan) , equipped with cutting-edge equipment and infrastructure to conduct essential research activities such as toxicology studies, field trials and residue analysis, scale-up studies, synthesis, and formulation development, as well as analytical chemistry.

Our strong regulatory affairs team excels in navigating the complex landscape of crop protection chemical regulations, both nationally and internationally. This expertise has allowed us to secure over 110 registrations in more than 25 countries, including diverse regions like Asia, Africa, LATAM and the Middle East.

GCS is a trusted supplier of Glyphosate and Glufosinate worldwide, and our product range spans insecticides, herbicides, fungicides, biostimulants, plant growth regulators, and more. We prioritize innovation, leveraging our in-house R&D capabilities and a dedicated team of agricultural specialists to continuously introduce new and unique combinations of formulations.

With fully equipped manufacturing facilities in Panipat, Haryana, and Ghiloth, Rajasthan, GCS ensures the highest quality standards in production. These facilities are ISO 9001:2008, ISO 14001:2004, and BS OHSAS 18001:2007 accredited, featuring segregated production lines for insecticides, herbicides, fungicides, and other products to maintain strict quality control and avoid cross-contamination.

At GCS, we are committed to quality assurance from seed to harvest. We subject all raw materials to rigorous testing, and each production batch undergoes meticulous testing at our ISO 17025 -accredited laboratory to ensure adherence to the highest quality standards.

In a rapidly evolving business environment, GCS embraces a culture of growth through innovation and skill enhancement. Please feel free to contact the email address provided below for any inquiries related to exports.

For Technicals & Pesticide Formulations please contact at export@rcs.in.net
