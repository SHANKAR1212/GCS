GENERAL CROP SCIENCE PVT. LTD.

(Technical/Contract Manufacturing Business)

  1. GCS operates a total of two plants dedicated to manufacturing agrochemical products.
  2. The combined plant capacity reaches an impressive 20,000 metric tons per annum, showcasing the company's ability to meet market demands effectively.
  3. GCS is involved in the manufacturing of a remarkable 300 active ingredients, demonstrating its commitment to delivering diverse and high-quality agrochemical solutions.
  4. The company imports 40 intermediates to support its production processes and maintain product quality.
  5. These manufacturing operations are strategically located in Haryana and Rajasthan, India, further enhancing GCS's reach and capabilities.
  6. The manufacturing facilities occupy a total area of 5.5 acres, with 11 production lines to ensure efficient and quality-driven production processes.

General Crop Science Pvt. Ltd. focuses on maintaining a strong manufacturing and R&D foundation to deliver superior agrochemical products and contribute to the agriculture industry's growth and sustainability.
